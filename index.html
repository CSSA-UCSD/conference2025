About

When we think of magic, what field of study instantly comes to mind? Cognitive Science, of course! What could be more magical than the human mind? The interdisciplinary interplay of neuroscience, linguistics, computer science, and all our specializations make for a magical mixture of endless learning opportunities and revolutionary innovations.

Immerse yourself in the magic at CSSA's 18th National cognitive science Conference, where top professionals in their fields explain their innovative research and students present their latest projects within the department. Become inspired, make some connections, and spark your own magic within.
